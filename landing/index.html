New Visitor Notification and Tracking

Visitors to your site are tracked at arrival and alerted to your device providing you important data like country of origin and usage statistics such as which pages they have formerly and are currently visiting and which elements on those pages have their most attention.

Immediate Engagement

Reactively and/or proactively communicate with your visitors to drive sales.

Reservation Inventory System

Yakchat includes a powerful reservation system that allows you to define your recurring reservation system years in advance with just a few clicks. Easily define black-out periods in the same way. A powerful system of options allows you to offer detailed customization to your customers during the reservation process.

Product Widgets

Easily markup your existing webpages containing your offerings to allow integration with Yakchats client-side reservation-system and checkout-itinerary

Checkout Itinerary

Your customers can easily define their entire trip using Yakchats checkout-itinerary which helps them organize their precious time and fill it with fantastic adventures at their own pace. Customers can markup the itinerary with their own notes/schedules to help them visualize their days.

Payment Gateway

Receive payments from your clients with convenient options of Paypal, Stripe and others with more coming online in the future.

Agent Pool

With our free version (which shall always remain free), you may operate as your own agent and close your own sales. Or, you may lean on our Agent pool - a group of competent expats who are passionate about tourism. They natively speak the language of your target customer and they know *all* about your products and offerings by training with you on your tours. More than that, because they operate on pure commission, they drive prospects to your offerings by advertising on the internet on your behalf via services like TripAdvisor.

Automated Disbursements

Accept deposits and/or full payments online with policies that allow you to define refundability. Portions of those payments can be automatically distributed to agents (on commission) and other partner providers.

Aggregated Offerings

To ensure that we are able to offer *all* a customer could possibly want, we are grouping together complementary services (never competing services) and offering them at our aggregated tour websites (like Adventure Tours Peru). We will combine your offerings with hotels, restaurants, guides, and other complementing tours that fills your customers itinerary for a vacation they will never forget. We maintain a friendship and follow up with our customers to ensure they leave us all fine reviews to further enhance our visibility and traffic to our websites.
